FreshFold Site Plan

Responsive Frontend Laundry Service Website

Site Name

FreshFold Laundry Co.

The name FreshFold was chosen because it captures the two core promises of any laundry service: clean, fresh clothes that are neatly folded and returned to the customer. It is short, memorable, and immediately communicates the service's value without needing further explanation. The word "Co." adds a professional, brand-like identity that builds customer trust.

Site Purpose

FreshFold Laundry Co. provides an interactive online platform where customers can learn about available laundry services, place orders, and track the progress of their laundry in real time. The site covers three primary areas: a homepage introducing services and pricing; an order form where customers submit their laundry request with details such as service type and quantity; and a customer dashboard that simulates real-time order tracking through five stages — Order Received, Washing in Progress, Drying Completed, Ready for Pickup, and Delivered — with on-screen notification popups at each stage transition. All order data is stored using the browser's localStorage, allowing the dashboard to persist and display order information across page navigation without requiring a backend server.

Scenarios

  • How can I track the current status of my laundry order?
  • What laundry services are available and how much do they cost?
  • When will my laundry be ready for pickup?
  • How can I contact the laundry service?
